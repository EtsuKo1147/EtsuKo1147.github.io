Fashion Storytelling

This project explores fashion imagery through a soft, editorial perspective. Rather than focusing only on clothing, the work highlights tone, environment and visual rhythm. The goal is to create images that feel elegant, modern and emotionally resonant — like stills from a contemporary magazine spread.
